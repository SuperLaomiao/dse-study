DSE_Study · Flow & Wireframe v1

Mobile-first family English learning system for you, your sister, and your mom as family admin.

Product Loop
Core Learning Cycle

1. Baseline Review

30-40 min onboarding assessment

  • Vocabulary
  • Reading
  • Writing
  • Listening
  • Speaking

2. Personal Plan

System assigns Reference Level + Internal Band

  • DSE track
  • Foundation-to-DSE
  • Companion track

3. Daily Session

40-60 min, 6 days a week

  • Vocabulary Loop
  • Reading / Listening
  • Writing / Speaking
  • Review due items

4. Bi-weekly Review

Every 14 days

  • Diagnostic review
  • Exam-style review
  • Band update

5. Adaptive Update

New focus for next cycle

  • Weak skill alerts
  • B1/B2 readiness
  • Family dashboard update
